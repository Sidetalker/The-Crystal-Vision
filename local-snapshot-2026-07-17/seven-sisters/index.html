What this is

CrystalCore is a personal creative protocol and storytelling system: Songlines-inspired paths, sky anchors (Pleiades / Orion as culture + astronomy), and Australian water themes held as care priorities.

It is art, documentation, and optional software — a way to organize vision and ship public work.
